Problem

  • Retail and pro traders lack a single platform for real-time data, options analytics, AI signals, and execution.
  • Fragmented tools → multiple subscriptions, no unified view of portfolio and risk.
  • Growing demand for transparent, AI-augmented decision-making in options and multi-asset trading.

Solution

Octopus (Findash) — all-in-one AI trading platform:

  • Options-first Trading Center — IV, Greeks, expirations, strategy tools.
  • 11 orchestrated AI agents — Data, Strategy, Sentiment, Risk, Analytics.
  • Unified dashboard — Portfolio, live data, bots, reports in one UI.
  • Security & control — Paper trading first; optional live execution with risk limits.
